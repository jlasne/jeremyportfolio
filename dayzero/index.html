DEFINITION
day zero
noun. the day the product ships.

The work you do before it exists. You market the product first, so a crowd is already waiting on the day it launches. Win day zero and you win the launch.

0m · TOPSOIL
START

A cold launch is
a dead launch.

Most founders treat marketing as something that starts on launch day. You ship, you post once, you wait. Then nothing happens, because nobody was there to hear it.

The whole job before launch is one sentence. Turn strangers into a waitlist that installs on day one. Everything below digs toward that.

-15m · ROCK
LAYER 01

Build an
organic empire.

The landing page is secretly a media company. It publishes like one and it ranks like one. The point is traffic that compounds on its own, so the site is a channel by launch, not a brochure.

  • ▸5 blog articles a day, built to rank for real search intent
  • ▸Daily articles on authority sites for reach and backlinks
  • ▸4 directory listings a day. Small links that add up to authority

None of this converts overnight. That is the point. Organic is the ore you mine early so it pays you for free, forever, after the work is done.

-22m · DEEP ROCK
LAYER 02

Own every
potential user.

Traffic you don't capture is traffic you rent. So every visitor has one job: get on the list. An algorithm can change overnight. An email list cannot be taken away.

  • ▸Build the waitlist now, long before the app is live
  • ▸The newsletter is fed by the blog as readers subscribe
  • ▸And fed by the app, where onboarding captures the email
-30m · BASALT
LAYER 03

Mass social
posting.

Social is not for conversion yet. It is for learning. Before launch I run every account hard to find the hook and the format that travels. Low conversion now buys a loud launch later.

  • ▸Warm up each account for 5 days, then 3 posts a day
  • ▸Test hooks and CTAs until the winners are obvious
  • ▸Grow every account and send the traffic to the waitlist
-38m · ORE SEAM
LAYER 04

Make friends
before launch.

The best launch-day reach is borrowed from people who already have an audience. You don't buy that on day one. You mine it now, quietly, one real relationship at a time.

  • ▸Reach out to micro influencers in the niche, early
  • ▸Build a real relationship, not a one-off ask
  • ▸Trust now buys reach on launch day
-46m · NEAR BEDROCK
LAYER 05 · GO LOUD

Launch day.
Go loud.

Everything above loads the spring. Launch day releases it all at once. The waitlist becomes installs, the CTAs switch from "join the list" to "get the app", and paid finally turns on behind the content that already proved itself.

  • ▸Every website and social CTA switches to the app
  • ▸Send the whole waitlist to the app
  • ▸Apple ads go live
  • ▸TikTok and Reddit ads run behind the top tested content
